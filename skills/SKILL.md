# AI Regulatory Knowledge Base Skill

Use this skill when the user asks about AI regulations, compliance frameworks, or needs regulatory reference material.

## When to use
- User asks about EU AI Act requirements, risk classifications, or timelines
- User asks about ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, or other AI standards
- User needs to compare regulatory requirements across jurisdictions
- User asks about AI compliance obligations in a specific country

## Available content

### Regulations (regulations/ directory)
- EU AI Act — Full text summaries, risk tiers, timelines, obligations
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework
- ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management System)
- Canada AIDA
- China AI regulations
- Brazil AI Act
- UK AI regulatory approach
- And more regional frameworks

### Visualizations
- World map of AI regulation status by country
- Regulation comparison matrices
- Timeline visualizations

## How to use
Read the relevant regulation files from the `regulations/` directory. Each file is structured with:
- Overview and scope
- Key requirements and obligations
- Risk classifications (where applicable)
- Implementation timelines
- Enforcement mechanisms
